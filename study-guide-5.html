Human Autonomy in the AI Age

Exploring how Artificial Intelligence reshapes our ability to choose, act, and organize society. Are we losing control, or enhancing it?

Human Autonomy

Self-Governance

The freedom to make decisions based on your own values without external manipulation.
Threat: AI algorithms subtly manipulating preferences (e.g., Netflix, TikTok).

Human Agency

Capacity to Act

The ability to execute choices and effect change in the world.
Impact: AI can enhance agency (new tools) or reduce it (dependency).

Psychological Effects of AI Reliance

The Psychology of Dependency

Automation Bias

The tendency to trust machines over our own judgment. We assume the AI is "objective" or "smarter," leading to errors when the AI hallucinates.

Skill Erosion

"Use it or lose it." As we offload cognitive tasks (navigation, writing, calculating) to AI, our own ability to perform these tasks degrades.
