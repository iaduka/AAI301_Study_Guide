About AAI301

Learn more about the Ethical and Societal Impact of Generative AI course

Course Overview

AAI301 Ethical and Societal Impact of Generative AI from SUSS explores the ethical considerations, societal implications, and governance frameworks surrounding generative AI technologies. This course equips students with the knowledge to navigate the complex landscape of AI ethics.
