The 2025 Evolutionary Leap

Why is regulation urgent now? Because AI is no longer just generating text. Between 2023 and 2025, we have witnessed a shift towards Agentic AI (autonomous action), Advanced Reasoning, and true Multimodality. The capabilities have expanded exponentially.

Reasoning

Models like OpenAI o1 now perform multistep problem-solving and nuanced analysis.

Agentic AI

Systems that don't just chat, but handle workflows (scheduling, coding) autonomously.

Multimodality

Seamless processing and generation of Text, Audio, Image, and Video simultaneously.

The Future: Promise vs. Peril

Thought leaders are starkly divided. Optimists like Mustafa Suleyman see abundance and health, while pessimists like Geoffrey Hinton warn of existential risks.

The Optimists

Suleyman, Hassabis

  • "Cure all diseases"
  • "Personal AI Companions"
  • "Radical Abundance"

The Pessimists

Hinton, Bengio

  • "10-20% chance of AI takeover"
  • "Human Extinction"
  • "Concentration of Power"

Optimists

Predict healthcare revolution and global abundance.
