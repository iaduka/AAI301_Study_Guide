Why Do We Believe?

It's not just the tech; it's our brains. Cognitive biases make us vulnerable to AI-generated falsehoods.

Confirmation Bias

We accept fakes that align with what we already want to believe.

Visual Supremacy

"Seeing is believing." Our brains are wired to trust visual input over text.

The Liar's Dividend

The mere existence of deepfakes allows bad actors to dismiss real evidence as fake.
